MapData (ScriptableObject) - Contains all information about a specific map
    -   Nodes           (Node[])
    -   Segments        (NodeSegment[])
    -   Board           (GameObject)
    -   MapTokens       (MapTokenTemplates)

MapTokenTemplates(ScriptableObject) - All template objects representing different pieces on a particular map
    -   Clear           (GameObject)
    -   Mountain        (GameObject)
    -   SmallCity       (GameObject)
    -   MediumCity      (GameObject)
    -   MajorCity       (GameObject)
    -   River           (? [Part of the map background?])
    -   Water           (? [Part of the map background?])

PlayerTokenTemplates(ScriptableObject) - All information related to a player's pieces on a map 
(decoupling from MapData allows mix and matching, based on player preference [if desired])
    -   TemplateName    (String) [ie. British Rail Class 150, Taiwan Railway EMU100 series, etc.]
    -   TrainToken      (GameObject)
    -   RailToken       (GameObject)
